About

Hey, I'm Star, the creator of Star Content Studio.

I help small, local, and remote businesses like bakeries, medspas, and neighborhood services feel confident about their social media instead of dreading it.

I know how it goes: you're busy running the actual business, and Instagram ends up being whatever you can squeeze in between everything else. That's where I come in. I plan the calendar, write the captions, put together the content, and keep things posted consistently, so your feed actually looks like you, and brings in more customers, not just likes.

My favorite part of this work is getting to know a business well enough that the content feels like it's coming from the owner, not a stranger managing an account. If that sounds like what you need, I'd love to help.
